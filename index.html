A little about me...
My name is Matthew Batko. I graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in Information Technology and Informatics in 2017. Today, I am working at Rutgers supporting online learning, but I believe web development is my calling.
I've learned a bit through self study, but the Rutgers Full Stack Bootcamp has helped bolster my talents and has made me a much more capable and confident developer.
Technologies...
I have knowledge of front end technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript including front end frameworks such as Bootstrap, jQuery, Ajax, and React. I've used these technologies to create responsive, interactive webpages.
I have extensive knowledge of several backend technologies, including Python 3, Node.js, Java, & PHP, including Laravel, plus several database technologies such as Oracle, MySQL, and MongoDB. I have a particular interest in Node.js for backend web development and Python 3 for just about anything else, especially automation. Be sure to check out my portfolio!
Some more about me...
Because of how much time I spend in front of a computer at work and through studying, I try to limit my time in front of screens in my free time. I like to spend a lot of time hiking and skiing, working out, playing sports, and, simply put, trying to live my life.
My favorite thing to do is travel. It can be a bit difficult to travel all the time, but in the past year I have traveled the North East US, and to Canada, Germany, Poland, and Switzerland.
